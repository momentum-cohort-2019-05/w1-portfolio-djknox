Lazy River Nursery and Garden Supply

Lazy River Nursey and Garden Supply started in 1976 and has been helping the Tri-State area tend to their gardens ever since.

Using HTML and CSS, I built a website that lets Lazy River display what items they have in stock and ensure their location and hours of operation are easy to find and up-to-date.

More importantly, the website allows Lazy River to communicate their mission and values with current and future customers and gives them a way to express their brand online.

Happy Valley Shelter

Happy Valley Shelter is an animal shelter that specializes in rescuing dogs and placing them with loving humans in happy homes.

Using HTML and CSS, I created a website that lets potential adopters find their perfect dog by filtering on various traits like gender, age, size, and personality.
